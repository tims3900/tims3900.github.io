About Me

I recently graduated from UC Irvine with a degree in Computer Science. I’m passionate about machine learning, particularly in how it can be applied to interdisciplinary fields. During my time at university, I had the opportunity to work in the Molloi Lab, a medical imaging research lab, where I explored how machine learning can detect early signs of diseases from CT scans. I also gained experience applying ML techniques to environmental science projects, including my internship with MBARI, where I worked on analyzing UAV images to measure kelp coverage. I hope to continue applying my skills to a variety of fields that combine computation, science, and real-world impact.
